### ❌ **New Content Issues** (Not Recommended)

1. **Keyword Stuffing Problems**

   - "Cash for Gold in Noida" repeated 15+ times
   - "Diamond Buyers in Delhi" mentioned excessively
   - Same phrases repeated verbatim across sections
   - Google penalty risk for over-optimization

2. **Poor Structure**

   - Walls of text without proper formatting
   - Missing proper heading hierarchy
   - No visual breaks or scannable elements
   - Difficult to read and navigate

3. **Repetitive Content**

   - Same information repeated in different words
   - No unique value in each section
   - Diluted messaging reduces SEO impact
   - User confusion from redundancy

4. **Length Issues**

   - 3,000+ words per page (excessive)
   - Average user reads only 20-28% of page content
   - Increases bounce rate
   - Slows page load time

5. **Mixed Location Content**
   - Combines Delhi, Noida, Lajpat Nagar in one document
   - Should be separate pages for local SEO
   - Confuses search engines about page focus
   - Reduces ranking potential for each location

## Content Guidelines for Future Updates

### ✅ **Do This:**

1. Keep pages between 800-1,500 words
2. Use proper heading hierarchy (H1 → H2 → H3)
3. Add 1-2 images per section
4. Include 3-5 internal links per page
5. Place CTAs every 300-400 words
6. Use bullet points for scanability
7. Write for humans first, search engines second
8. Include local landmarks and areas served
9. Add trust indicators (years in business, certifications)
10. Update content quarterly with new information

### ❌ **Don't Do This:**

1. Repeat the same phrases more than 2-3 times
2. Write walls of text without breaks
3. Create pages over 2,000 words
4. Mix multiple locations on one page
5. Use excessive keywords (over 3% density)
6. Copy content between pages
7. Bury CTAs at the bottom only
8. Ignore mobile users (50%+ of traffic)
9. Skip internal linking opportunities
10. Leave outdated information (rates, offers, dates)
