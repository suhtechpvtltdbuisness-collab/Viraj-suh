# Internal Linking Strategy for SEO

## Implementation Guide

### Priority Internal Links Added

#### Homepage (/)

- Links to all major service pages
- Links to blog posts about cash for gold
- Links to location pages
- Links to gold rates page
- Strategic placement in content sections

#### Service Pages

Each service page should link to:

- Related services (cross-linking)
- Relevant blog posts
- Location pages
- Homepage and contact page
- Gold rates page

#### Blog Posts

Each blog post includes:

- Links to service pages (contextual)
- Links to other related blog posts
- Links to contact/call-to-action pages
- Links to location pages

#### Location Pages

- Link to all services available in that location
- Link to relevant blog posts
- Link to gold rates
- Link to testimonials

### Anchor Text Strategy

**Primary Keywords (use sparingly - 5-10% of links):**

- "Cash for gold in Lajpat Nagar Delhi"
- "Best gold buyer in Lajpat Nagar"
- "Sell gold in Delhi"

**Branded (20-30%):**

- "Viraj Jewellers"
- "Our services"
- "Visit us"

**Natural/Conversational (60-70%):**

- "Learn more about gold selling"
- "Read our complete guide"
- "Check today's gold rates"
- "See how we evaluate gold"
- "Discover our transparent process"

### Link Distribution

**Footer Links (global navigation):**

- About Us
- Services
- Locations
- Blog
- FAQ
- Testimonials
- Contact
- Gold Rates Today

**Contextual Links (within content):**

- At least 3-5 internal links per page
- Naturally integrated into content
- Relevant anchor text
- Opens in same tab (not \_blank)

**Breadcrumb Navigation:**

- Implement on all pages
- Format: Home > Category > Page
- Schema.org BreadcrumbList markup

### Implementation Priority

**Phase 1 (Critical):**

1. ✅ Footer navigation complete
2. ✅ Service pages cross-linking
3. ✅ Blog post internal links
4. ✅ Homepage strategic links

**Phase 2 (Important):** 5. Breadcrumb implementation 6. Related posts section in blog 7. "You may also like" sections 8. Category archive pages

**Phase 3 (Enhancement):** 9. Dynamic related content based on tags 10. Popular posts sidebar 11. Recently viewed items 12. Seasonal promotions links

### Metrics to Track

- Internal link click-through rate
- Pages per session (should increase)
- Average session duration (should increase)
- Bounce rate (should decrease)
- Page authority distribution

### Best Practices

1. **Relevance:** Only link to genuinely related content
2. **Balance:** Don't over-link (3-5 per page ideal)
3. **Natural:** Make links flow naturally in content
4. **Descriptive:** Use descriptive anchor text
5. **Hierarchy:** Link from high-authority pages to lower ones
6. **Update:** Regularly audit and update broken links

### Tools for Monitoring

- Google Search Console: Internal Links Report
- Screaming Frog SEO Spider: Site crawl
- Ahrefs/SEMrush: Internal link analysis
- Google Analytics: Behavior Flow
